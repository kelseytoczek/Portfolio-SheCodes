This is the first web development project I created. When creating this project, I chose something that I am extremely passionate about - photography.

My favorite type of photography is street photography. I love the candid nature of it. I also prefer the look and feel of film photography - although it is not a cheap hobby. To save money on film, I try to emulate the look of film photography by using editing tools like Adobe Lightroom.

With this webpage I used more advanced web development techniques and tools to build my interactive vanilla JS weather webpage.
